04 · Quantum

Quantum Entanglement

  • Quantum entanglement is a phenomenon where two or more particles can become correlated in such a way that the state of one particle depends on the state of the other, even if the particles are separated by a large distance
  • This correlation is not explained by classical physics and is one of the key features of quantum mechanics
